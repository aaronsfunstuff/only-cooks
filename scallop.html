Scallops with Cauliflower Puree

Prep Time: 10 minutes

Cook Time: 20 minutes

Difficulty: Medium

Servings: 2

Ingredients

  • 8 large sea scallops
  • 1 small head of cauliflower, chopped
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper
  • Chopped chives for garnish

Instructions

  1. In a large pot, bring water to a boil and add the cauliflower. Cook until tender, about 15 minutes. Drain and transfer to a blender.
  2. Add the heavy cream and butter to the cauliflower. Blend until smooth. Season with salt and pepper. Keep warm.
  3. Heat olive oil in a skillet over high heat. Season the scallops with salt and pepper. Sear the scallops for 2-3 minutes per side until golden brown and cooked through.
  4. To serve, spoon the cauliflower puree onto plates and top with the seared scallops. Garnish with chopped chives.

Nutrition Facts

Nutrient Amount (per serving)
Calories 300 kcal
Protein 22 g
Fat 20 g
Carbohydrates 15 g
Fiber 4 g

Tips

Ensure your scallops are very dry before searing them to get a perfect crust. Pat them dry with paper towels and avoid overcrowding the pan.
