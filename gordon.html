About Gordon Ramsay

Gordon James Ramsay OBE is a British chef, restaurateur, writer, and television personality. His restaurant group, Gordon Ramsay Restaurants, was founded in 1997 and has been awarded 16 Michelin stars in total and currently holds seven. His signature restaurant, Restaurant Gordon Ramsay in Chelsea, London, has held three Michelin stars since 2001. Ramsay rose to fame on the British television miniseries Boiling Point in 1999, becoming one of the best-known and most influential chefs in the UK.
